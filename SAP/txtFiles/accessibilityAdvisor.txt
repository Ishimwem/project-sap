Our professional Accessibility Advisors are experts in the areas you identified in your registration package. They will recommend appropriate academic accommodation and support for you. They will discuss with you the impact that your situation has on your academic career, and introduce you to the services we provided.
<br>
Email our Accessibility Advisors for questions, inquiries and appointments. Drop in hours are also available for you to meet them on campus. Click on contact button to see schedules and contacts.
